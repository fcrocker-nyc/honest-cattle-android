THE HONEST CATTLE APP — Your Ranch in Your Pocket

The Honest Cattle App puts market prices, weather, moisture, and your whole herd record one tap away — built for ranchers, glove-friendly, and ready for the chute or the pickup seat. Available for iPhone and Android.

  • 📊 Live cattle market — futures, grids, and Billings auction prices
  • ⛅ Localized ranch weather and a weekly Honest Cattle forecast
  • 💧 County moisture and drought tracking from SNOTEL data
  • 🐌 Full herd records — animals, calving, and treatments, all offline-first
  • 🧮 Ranch tools — breakeven, dosage, and weight-estimate calculators

📱 iPhone

The Honest Cattle App is a free download on the Apple App Store. No account required. Your herd records stay on your device.

🪨 Android

The Honest Cattle App is available for Android as a direct download — no app store required. Install the APK directly on your device. Free. No account required.
